# Adding Knowledge to LLMs

Lecturers: Alexandros , Guillelme 

Methods:
* prompting
* fine-tuning:
    * full fine-tuning
    * parameter-efficient fine-tuning (PEFT)
* retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)
* agent-based approaches

Different types of fine-tuning:
* domain-adaptive fine-tuning: adapt the model to a specific domain (e.g. medical, legal, etc.)
* instruction fine-tuning: adapt the model to follow instructions (e.g. InstructGPT, FLAN, etc.)
* reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF): adapt the model to follow human preferences
